# Panasonic Grid-Eye on Teensy 3.2
Softwares: Arduino & Processing.

# Schema.
See the .jpeg

# Arduino IDE & Teensyduino.
1. Download the lastest version of Arduino.
Arduino official site: https://www.arduino.cc/
2. Download Teensyduino. 
Teensyduino: https://www.pjrc.com/teensy/td_download.html
3. Load the sketch grid-eye.ino in Arduino. 
4. Follow the schema, and connect your Teensy to your computer. Verify that Arduino IDE recognizes it ('Tools').
5. Implement the sketch.
6. Data can be shown with Arduino serial monitoring.

# Processing.
1. Download Processing v2.0 on the official site.
Processing official site:  https://processing.org/
2. Connect Arduino IDE and Processing. Just follow the steps on the link below.
Tuto: http://playground.arduino.cc/Interfacing/Processing
3. Load the sketch gui.pde in Processing.
4. Just run it, and enjoy!
